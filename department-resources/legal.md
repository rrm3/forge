# Legal - AI Tuesdays Resources

## Key Focus Areas
* Data Privacy Laws & EU AI Act
* Implementing and supervising legal automation workflows
* AI tool evaluation and governance

## Use Cases

**Trialing legal review tools**
* Evaluate tools like Harvey, CoCounsel, or Lexis+ AI for contract review and legal research
* Start with low-risk document types: NDAs, standard vendor contracts
* Build an evaluation rubric before trialing: accuracy, citation quality, hallucination rate
* Document what the tool gets right and where human review is still required

**General productivity via Gemini**
* Use Gemini in Google Workspace for drafting correspondence, summarizing documents, and research
* Ask AI to summarize a long contract and pull out key obligations, dates, and risk flags
* Draft legal communications faster - use AI for the first pass, apply your judgment on top

**AI as a team member for specific responsibilities**
* Assign AI specific monitoring tasks: "flag any contract clauses that conflict with our standard data processing agreement"
* Use AI to track regulatory changes - paste updates from ICO, EU AI Act guidance, etc. and ask for impact analysis
* Build a prompt that checks any new vendor agreement against your data privacy requirements

## Getting Started
* Start with Gemini for daily productivity - it's low-risk and already available
* Pick one document type you review repeatedly and test whether AI pre-review saves time
* Read the EU AI Act summary and ask AI to explain your organization's likely obligations

## Recommended Tools
* Gemini (Google Workspace): Day-to-day drafting and document review
* Claude: Nuanced legal analysis, policy review, risk flagging
* Harvey / CoCounsel: Specialist legal AI tools (evaluate before committing)
