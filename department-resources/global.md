# Global AI Resources - All Departments

## Learning Tracks
Structured learning paths available through AI Tuesdays. Pick the one that matches where you are and where you want to go.

* **AI Leaders track:** For managers and senior ICs who need to make decisions about AI adoption, governance, and team enablement
* **Prompt Engineering track:** Core skills for getting reliable, high-quality output from any AI tool - relevant to every role
* **Google Workspace track:** Practical AI use within Gemini, Docs, Gmail, Sheets, and Meet - low barrier, high immediate value
* **Gen AI track:** Broader understanding of generative AI capabilities, limitations, and where it's heading
* **Agentic AI track:** Building and managing AI agents and automated workflows - for those ready to go beyond prompting

## Getting Started with AI
Every role at Digital Science can benefit from AI. Here are universal starting points regardless of your department.

### Prompt Engineering Basics
* **Be specific:** Instead of "help me write an email", try "Write a professional email to a client explaining a 2-week delay on deliverable X, keeping the tone collaborative"
* **Give context:** Tell the AI your role, audience, and constraints
* **Iterate:** Treat the first response as a draft. Refine by asking for changes.
* **Use examples:** Show the AI what good output looks like

### Quick Wins for Any Role
* **Meeting notes:** Paste meeting transcripts and ask for structured summaries with action items
* **Email drafting:** Draft responses to complex emails with the right tone
* **Document review:** Ask AI to review documents for clarity, consistency, or gaps
* **Research synthesis:** Summarize multiple sources on a topic
* **Calendar optimization:** Use AI to block focus time and batch similar meetings

### Recommended Tools
* **Claude (claude.ai):** Best for nuanced writing, analysis, and coding. Excellent at following complex instructions.
* **ChatGPT (chat.openai.com):** Good all-rounder. Strong at creative tasks and data analysis with Code Interpreter.
* **Microsoft Copilot:** Integrated into Office 365 - useful for Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Teams.

### Data Safety Guidelines
* Never share personal data of customers or employees with external AI tools
* Don't share financial data, credentials, or passwords
* Internal business strategies and unannounced plans should stay internal
* When in doubt, ask your manager or the security team
