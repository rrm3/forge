# Product - AI Tuesdays Resources

## Key Focus Areas
* AI Design & UX
* AI Prototyping
* Design Thinking
* Agentic Workflow Management
* Product Mindset
* Visual Analytics

## Use Cases

**Accelerated product discovery and synthesis**
* Paste user interview transcripts and ask AI to surface themes, tensions, and unmet needs
* Feed in support tickets, NPS comments, or feedback forms - ask for patterns by segment
* Use AI to draft "how might we" statements from raw research findings
* Ask AI to challenge your assumptions: "what would need to be true for this to fail?"

**Rapid prototyping and technical feasibility**
* Describe a feature concept and ask AI to outline technical approaches and trade-offs
* Use AI to generate user story maps and acceptance criteria from a problem statement
* Ask AI to review a PRD for ambiguity, gaps, and edge cases before it reaches engineering
* Build quick concept prototypes with AI-generated specs to test ideas faster

**Consolidating user feedback into product features**
* Set up a recurring prompt: paste your latest feedback batch and ask for feature signal vs noise
* Ask AI to map feedback themes against your current roadmap - what's covered, what's not
* Generate a ranked list of feature candidates from a large feedback data set with reasoning

## Getting Started
* Take your next user research session and run the transcript through Claude for synthesis
* Ask AI to review your next PRD before sharing it with engineering

## Recommended Tools
* Claude: Research synthesis, spec writing, feasibility analysis, strategic thinking
* ChatGPT with Code Interpreter: Data analysis, usage charts
* Figma AI: Design prototyping and iteration
