# Customer Solutions - AI Tuesdays Resources

## Key Focus Areas
* Tool integration and automations
* Agentic workflow management
* Knowledge sharing across teams and timezones

## Use Cases

**Converting colleague reviews**
* Use AI to transform internal peer reviews, feedback notes, or solution evaluations into structured deliverables
* Paste unstructured review notes and ask AI to produce a summary, scorecard, or recommendation memo
* Useful for turning fast async feedback into something that can be shared with clients or leadership

**Self-service for sales teams on past work**
* Build a searchable knowledge base of past solutions, proposals, and case studies
* Sales can query it with natural language: "show me work we've done for mid-market SaaS companies with this problem"
* Talk to Technology about a RAG setup - this is achievable and high-value for a solutions team

**Small working groups: pairing advanced and less experienced users**
* Structure AI learning in pairs: one person who's used AI heavily + one who hasn't
* Advanced user demonstrates a workflow, less experienced user replicates it in a different context
* Works better than general training - hands-on, immediately applicable
* Document what each pair figures out and add it to a shared prompt library

**Answering questions across teams and timezones**
* Use AI to generate thorough, well-sourced answers to cross-team queries that can be async
* Build a Gem or custom Claude Project with context about your solutions portfolio - reduces repeated Q&A
* When someone asks a question that an AI could answer reliably, route it there first

## Getting Started
* Identify your 3 most commonly asked internal questions - can AI answer them consistently?
* Start the pairing program: find 2-3 advanced AI users who can run the first sessions

## Recommended Tools
* Claude Projects: Custom context for your solutions portfolio
* Gemini Gems: Persistent AI agents for specific team workflows
* Notion AI / Confluence AI: Knowledge base search and synthesis (if already in use)
