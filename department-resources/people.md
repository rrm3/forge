# People - AI Tuesdays Resources

## Key Focus Areas
* AI Ethics & Psychological Safety in the AI Era
* Change Management
* Process Optimisation
* Sentiment Analysis
* Data Analytics
* Product Mindset & Design Thinking
* Strategic Thinking

## Use Cases

**AI-enabled line manager capability**
* Build a prompt library for managers: difficult conversations, performance feedback, development planning
* Ask AI to review a manager's draft feedback and flag anything that could land poorly
* Generate scenario-based coaching materials tailored to real situations your managers face

**Employee query and policy resolution**
* AI can draft first-pass answers to HR policy questions - reduces tier-1 query volume
* Build a simple FAQ bot grounded in your actual policies (talk to Technology about RAG)
* Test it on your 20 most common employee questions before rolling out

**Employment law proactive insights**
* Paste recent employment law updates and ask AI to summarize impact for your organization
* Use AI to draft a briefing for the business when legislation changes
* Ask AI to flag where your current policies may need review against new requirements

**Predictive analytics for strategic enablement**
* Paste engagement survey comments and ask for sentiment themes and patterns
* Ask AI to generate action plan options based on survey findings
* Use AI to synthesize exit interview data across a quarter - look for systemic themes

**Administrative support**
* Draft onboarding materials, policy documents, and company announcements
* Automate repetitive comms: same message, different audiences - ask AI to reframe for each

## Getting Started
* Start with the prompt library for managers - it's low-risk and immediately useful
* Pick one recurring policy query and build a tested AI response for it

## Recommended Tools
* Claude: Sensitive communications, policy drafting, nuanced analysis, strategy
* Gemini (Google Workspace): Integrated into Docs/Gmail for day-to-day drafting
