# People (HR) - AI Tuesdays Resources

## Getting Started
People teams can use AI to draft communications, analyze survey data, streamline processes, and create better employee experiences. AI is a powerful tool for the human-centered work you do.

## Common Use Cases

### Recruitment
* Draft job descriptions from role requirements
* Try: Describe the role and team, ask AI for a compelling job posting
* Generate interview questions tailored to specific competencies

### Employee Communications
* Draft company announcements, policy updates, and onboarding materials
* Try: Describe the change and audience, ask AI for a clear, empathetic announcement
* Advanced: Create personalized onboarding guides based on role and department

### Data Analysis
* Analyze engagement survey results and identify themes
* Try: Paste survey comments and ask for sentiment analysis and key themes
* Advanced: Generate action plans based on survey findings

## Recommended Tools
* Claude: Sensitive communications, policy drafting, nuanced analysis
* ChatGPT: Quick drafting, brainstorming
