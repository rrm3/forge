# Chief of Staff - AI Tuesdays Resources

## Key Focus Areas
* ESG Knowledge
* Meeting Synthesis
* Sentiment Mining
* AI-driven Business Intelligence
* Strategy Development

## Use Cases

**Building a Gem for market intelligence**
* Create a dedicated Gemini Gem (custom AI agent) configured for your market intelligence workflow
* Feed it your key competitors, industry sources, and strategic priorities - it learns your context
* Use it to synthesize news, analyst reports, and competitive moves into a weekly intelligence brief
* Ask it to flag signals that warrant leadership attention vs background noise

**Virtual avatars and async communication**
* Explore AI-generated video avatars (e.g., HeyGen) for internal briefings, strategy updates, and L&Ls
* Pre-record key messages as AI avatar clips - useful for distributed teams across timezones
* Test the format on a low-stakes internal update before using it for anything external

**Thought leadership in lunch-and-learns**
* Use AI to synthesize AI Tuesdays content into engaging session formats
* Ask AI to build a narrative arc for a topic - what's the story, not just the facts
* Generate facilitation questions, polls, and discussion prompts for each session

**Sentiment mining and strategy support**
* Paste employee survey data, meeting notes, or leadership feedback and ask for sentiment themes
* Ask AI to surface tensions or patterns across qualitative data you're tracking
* Use AI to stress-test strategic options: "what are the strongest arguments against this approach?"

## Getting Started
* Build your first Gem this week - market intelligence is the highest-value starting point
* Ask AI to synthesize your last 3 board meeting notes into a single strategic themes summary

## Recommended Tools
* Gemini with Gems: Custom agents for recurring intelligence workflows
* Claude: Strategy analysis, synthesis, nuanced interpretation
* HeyGen / Synthesia: AI video avatars for async communications (trial first)
