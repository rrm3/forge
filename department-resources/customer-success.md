# Customer Success - AI Tuesdays Resources

## Key Focus Areas
* Process Optimisation & Agentic Workflow Management
* Customer Learning & Customer Journeys
* Sentiment Analysis
* AI Learning Design
* Human-in-the-loop design

## Use Cases

**Ticket triage and FAQ responses**
* Use AI to categorize and prioritize incoming tickets before they reach the queue
* Build human-in-the-loop responses for high-frequency FAQs: AI drafts, human approves and sends
* Connect Front's AI features to your existing templates - reduce handling time on repeatable queries

**Task and process automation with Front and Gemini**
* Map which Front workflows are rule-based and automatable - these are your first targets
* Use Gemini to draft responses from within Gmail/Front for faster handling
* Build automation triggers in Front for routing, tagging, and first-response drafts

**Knowledge base optimization**
* Ask AI to review your existing knowledge base articles for gaps, outdated content, and clarity
* Paste your most common ticket types and ask AI to generate or improve KB articles for each
* Use AI to identify which KB articles would deflect the most tickets if they were easier to find

**Predictive churn analysis**
* Feed AI a structured summary of at-risk accounts and ask it to identify common early warning patterns
* Use AI to generate a churn risk scoring framework based on your customer health signals
* Ask AI to draft re-engagement campaign messaging tailored to each churn reason

**AI-assisted relationship summaries**
* Before a QBR or renewal call, ask AI to synthesize recent ticket history, usage data, and notes into a briefing
* Generate hyper-personalized re-engagement emails based on specific account context

## Getting Started
* Audit your top 20 ticket types - which ones have predictable answers? Start automating those.
* Turn on Front's AI features and spend one week testing them on real tickets

## Recommended Tools
* Front AI: Ticket drafting, routing, and automation
* Gemini (Google Workspace): Drafting and synthesis within your existing tools
* Claude: Complex relationship summaries, churn analysis, KB content generation
